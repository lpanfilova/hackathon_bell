Plastic Pollution




What is the problem?

The problem of using plastic is much more dramatic and harmfol than you think. People produce more than 380 million tonnes of plastic every year and only 16 % of this plastic is going to be recycled. We use it every day, like bottles, shopping bags, packages for all kinds of goods, but we aren’t informed enough about its harm.

Why is plastic bad?

  1. It takes up to 500 years to recycle.
  2. Plastic is polluting our environment
  3. It could even destroy the natural habits of all kinds of animals (mainly in the ocean)
  4. Big amount of animals are dying, because of getting stuck in the plastic

What could you do?

It’s really easy to get rid of plastic addiction:
  1. Stop using plastic bags while shopping. You coold buy a good cotton shopping bag for only 20$ and then use it for several years. By using this idea you save up to 5 kilograms of plastic for the next 2-3 years.
  2. Stop using plastic bottles. You coold buy a glass water bottle for 30$ and it will save up to 2 kilograms of plastic for the next 2-3 years
